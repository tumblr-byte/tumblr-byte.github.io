Vehicle Detection & Number Plate Recognition

This project uses computer vision to detect multiple types of vehicles such as cars, motorcycles, and trucks in real time. After detection, the system extracts the number plate (license plate) and recognizes the characters, displaying them automatically.

Business Benefits:

  • Traffic Management: Monitor traffic flow and identify vehicle types in real time.
  • Law Enforcement: Automatically detect and log number plates for speeding, red-light violations, or stolen vehicles.
  • Toll Collection: Enable automated toll booths without requiring manual checks.
  • Parking Systems: Manage smart parking lots by logging vehicle entry and exit with license plate recognition.
  • Fleet Management: Track commercial vehicles for logistics and transportation companies.
